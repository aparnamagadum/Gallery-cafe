About us

Welcome to Gallery Cafe,where each meal is a gastronomic adventure and each dish narrates a tale.Located in the centre of Bangalore's BTM Layout, our restaurant serves as more than simply a place to eat but it's a haven for food enthusiasts, a haven for those looking for flavours they won't soon forget, and a place where friends and family can come together to make lifelong memories.wonderful food, in our opinion at Gallery Cafe, begins with wonderful ingredients. To get the best, freshest produce and proteins possible, we collaborate with regional farmers, fishers, and craftspeople. Every component we use, from humanely grown meats and responsibly sourced seafood to farm-fresh veggies, is carefully selected to balance sustainability with flavour.
